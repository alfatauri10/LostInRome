About Us

LostInRome: when the passion for Rome meets student talent, it gives life to a digital tour guide that makes the monuments speak, in two languages and from the heart

Our Big Mission

Our mission is to offer a tourist guide designed for those visiting Rome: each page is dedicated to an iconic monument of the capital, accompanied by images, videos, detailed descriptions, and interesting facts. All of this is available in both Italian and English, allowing foreign tourists to immerse themselves in the history and culture of Rome.

Our Story

With great enthusiasm, this class, composed of 18 students, presents LostInRome, a website born from the combination of our passion for our city and the desire to experiment. We are capable students who work daily to create this site, and we do so in a fun and playful way to make everything more beautiful and enjoyable.

The breakthrough

The idea was born during a school trip to the center of Rome: each group of students had prepared to explain a monument in English to tourists, just as if they were real tour guides.

The project was entirely created by the class, divided into groups, but under the name of Unreal Team, our internal company. Each group worked on one or more monuments, taking care of research, translation, writing the texts, and developing the site.
